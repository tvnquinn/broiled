BROiled: An iPhone App Designed to Remind You To Work Out

Twelve screens covering the true MVP loop: set a schedule once, get counted down to every day, snooze if you must, get roasted or grudgingly congratulated, and go dark for a week if you don't show up. Push Notifications as reminders.
