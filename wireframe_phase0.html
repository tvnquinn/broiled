The collection page (Settings → the Burn Book). Chronological history of every line actually thrown - date + line + W/L chip. Unlock trackers count distinct lines against the surfaceable pools (36 roasts, 10 compliments; notification-body pools are excluded since delivery can't be observed). Badge ladder is itself insults: roasts 5 = certified punching bag, 10 = glutton for punishment, 25 = roast magnet, 30 = well done, all = fully roasted; compliments 3 = barely tolerable, 7 = annoyingly consistent, all = too hot to roast (the reserved easter egg becomes the crown badge). Thresholds are tuned down from the plan's 50/5/10 to stay attainable with the real pool sizes. This also finally surfaces the snooze escalation lines (shown under the countdown after snoozing), the success alternates, and the reactivation jabs - all previously written but unreachable.
